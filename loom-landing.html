Feature Deep Dive

Built layer by layer

Every feature is intentional. Every step moves you closer to someone real.

01

Swipe Right, They Know

The moment you swipe right, Loom sends an anonymous "someone is interested" notification to the other person. They don't know who — yet. This creates a beautiful loop of curiosity that makes them visit more profiles, including yours.

  • Anonymous interest notification sent instantly
  • Recipient sees "1 person is interested in you"
  • Mutual swipe = match with bonus XP
  • Drives organic profile visits and real attention

02

Interactive Q&A After Match

After matching, both users receive the same 3 questions — answered simultaneously, revealed together. It's a shared moment that breaks the ice without awkwardness and shows character before conversation.

  • Questions change weekly — always fresh, never stale
  • Both answer before seeing each other's response
  • Answers form a shared "connection card"
  • Great openers are auto-suggested based on answers

03

Schedule Sync & Time Windows

Both users share general availability — morning, afternoon, evening — for each day of the week. Loom surfaces the overlap and suggests a "connection window" — a natural time for a call, voice note, or real-world date.

  • No full calendar access — just simple time slots
  • Loom finds mutual free windows automatically
  • Built-in voice/video call booking within the app
  • Reminder nudge when window approaches
